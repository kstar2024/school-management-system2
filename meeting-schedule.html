Frequently Asked Questions

How can I view the meeting schedule?

The meeting schedule is displayed on this page. Check the table for upcoming meetings.

How can I schedule a new meeting?

How can I schedule a new meeting?

You can schedule a new meeting using the form provided above. Fill in the date, time, type, participants, location, and details of the meeting, then submit the form.

Can I request changes to an existing meeting?

If you need to request changes to an existing meeting, please contact the meeting organizer or email us at meetings@greatvalueacademy.com.

How do I find out who the meeting organizer is?

The meeting organizer's contact information can be found in the meeting details. For additional questions, you can reach out to meetings@greatvalueacademy.com.

What should I do if I cannot attend a scheduled meeting?

If you cannot attend a scheduled meeting, please notify the organizer as soon as possible. You can find their contact details in the meeting schedule or reach out to meetings@greatvalueacademy.com.
