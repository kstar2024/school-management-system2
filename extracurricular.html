Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for extracurricular activities?

You can sign up for extracurricular activities using the registration form above or contact the respective activity coordinator.

Are there any fees for participating in extracurricular activities?

Some activities may have associated fees for materials or events. Please check with the activity coordinator for details.

Can my child participate in more than one activity?

Yes, students can participate in multiple activities as long as their schedule permits and they meet the requirements for each activity.

What if my child is interested in an activity not listed here?

If your child is interested in an activity not currently offered, please contact us at activities@greatvalueacademy.com with your suggestions.

How can I get more information about a specific activity?

For more information about specific activities, please reach out to the contact email listed for each activity on this page.
