Frequently Asked Questions

How can I stay updated with school news?

You can stay updated with school news by visiting this page regularly. You can also follow us on social media for updates.

Can I submit news or announcements for the school?

Yes, you can submit news or announcements using the form above. Your submission will be reviewed before being published.

Who can submit news or announcements?

Parents, staff, and students are encouraged to submit news or announcements. Please use the form provided to share updates with the school community.

How long does it take for a news submission to be published?

News submissions are reviewed by the school administration. The review process usually takes 2-3 business days before the news is published.

Can I edit or delete my news submission?

Once a news submission is published, it cannot be edited or deleted by the submitter. If you need changes, please contact the school administration.

How do I contact the school administration?

You can contact the school administration via the contact form on the Contact page or by calling the school office.
