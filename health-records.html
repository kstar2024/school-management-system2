Frequently Asked Questions

How can I view my child's health records?

You can view your child's health records in the table provided on this page. If you have any concerns, please contact the school nurse.

How do I submit a new health record?

To submit a new health record, please fill out the form above with the required details and click "Submit Health Record".

<

What should I include in the health record submission?

Include your child's name, date of birth, any known health issues, current medications, doctor's name, and contact information. You can also add additional notes if necessary.

Can I update an existing health record?

Yes, you can update an existing health record by contacting the school nurse or using the "Update Record" link next to your child’s record.

Who can access my child's health records?

Health records are confidential and can only be accessed by authorized school personnel, including the school nurse and relevant administrative staff.

How often should health records be updated?

Health records should be updated whenever there are significant changes to your child’s health status or medication. Please review and update records at least once a year.
