Frequently Asked Questions

How can I view the weekly menu?

The weekly menu is available on this page. You can also download the PDF versions of the nutritional information for more details.

Can I request a specific meal for my child?

Meal requests are not accommodated on an individual basis. However, if you have dietary restrictions or allergies, please contact the cafeteria manager to discuss options.

How do I provide feedback about the cafeteria services?

You can provide feedback using the form above. We value your input and use it to improve our services.

What are the cafeteria's operating hours?

The cafeteria is open from 7:30 AM to 2:30 PM on weekdays. Breakfast is served from 7:30 AM to 8:00 AM, lunch from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM, and dinner from 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM.

Are there vegetarian or gluten-free options available?

Yes, we offer vegetarian and gluten-free meal options. Please inform the cafeteria staff of any dietary needs or restrictions when you visit.

How can I get in touch with the cafeteria manager?

You can reach the cafeteria manager through the contact form on the Contact page or by calling the cafeteria directly during operating hours.
